The Sip & Smile "Gathering Room"

Welcome to our cozy Gathering Room, the perfect spot for friends and family to come together and create lasting memories. Whether you're planning a casual get-together, celebrations, or a business meeting, our Gathering Room offers a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Features:

  • Comfortable Seating: Plush chairs and sofas to ensure everyone feels at home.
  • Ambiance: Soft lighting and charming decor to set the perfect mood.
  • Private Space: A secluded area for more intimate gatherings.
  • Private Space: A secluded area for more intimate gatherings
  • Amenities: Complimentary Wi-Fi, power outlets, and a selection of board games and books.

Menu Highlights:

Delicious Beverages: A variety of freshly brewed hot and cold coffee drinks.

Booking Information:

  • Reservations: We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred date and time.
  • Capacity: The Gathering Room can accommodate up to 20 guests comfortably.

Contact Us: For more information or to make a reservation, please contact us at contact@sipandsmile.com or call us at 03-123-4567 .
